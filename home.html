Data Science Conference India 2022

Data Science Conference India 2022 brings together India's leading Data Science and AI experts and practitioners to share their ideas and experience about data science tools, advanced development in this sphere and gives the attendees a first look at new trends & developer products. The conference will give attendees direct access to top Data Scientists from leading tech companies from all over India

Covid 19 Safety & Wellbeing

The safety and wellbeing of our attendees and staff is our number one priority. To give peace of mind, and reduce the need for face masks, attendees will be required to provide proof of double vaccination certificate or a time and date-stamped negative Covid test within 48 hours of the morning of the event. Without this proof attendees will not be admitted. In addition to this we and the venue will be ensuring that heightened cleaning procedures, social distancing and mask wearing in crowded areas will take place.
